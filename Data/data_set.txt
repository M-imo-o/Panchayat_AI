✅ Keywords: sc st certificate, caste certificate, reservation
✅ Purpose: Provides legal recognition of Scheduled Caste or Scheduled Tribe status for availing reservation benefits in education and employment.
✅ Eligibility: Persons belonging to recognized SC/ST communities in Kerala.
✅ Documents required for application: Proof of Caste (old records if available), Parent's caste certificate (if available), Residential proof.
✅ Fees and extra late fees: Through Akshaya: ₹25 (General). No late fees.
✅ Timeline for processing: 5-7 Working Days.
✅ Validity: Permanent.
✅ Website for application and extra information: https://edistrict.kerala.gov.in/
✅ Common features: Essential for affirmative action benefits. It is a one-time process, and the certificate remains valid throughout life.
✅ Procedure: 1. Submit application via e-District. 2. Verification of community status. 3. Approval by Village Officer. 4. Download/Print.

✅ Service: Widow Certificate
✅ Category: Social Certificate
✅ Keywords: widow, dependent certificate, survivor
✅ Purpose: Official confirmation of widowhood status for availing welfare benefits and pension schemes.
✅ Eligibility: Widows of deceased persons, residing in the locality.
✅ Documents required for application: Death certificate of husband, Marriage certificate (if available), [Redacted] card, Ration card.
✅ Fees and extra late fees: Through Akshaya: ₹25 (General). No late fees.
✅ Timeline for processing: 5-7 Working Days.
✅ Validity: Continuous (subject to eligibility).
✅ Website for application and extra information: https://edistrict.kerala.gov.in/
✅ Common features: Often required for accessing schemes like Widow Pension and other financial support systems.
✅ Procedure: 1. Apply via e-District portal. 2. Submit proof of death and identity. 3. Verification by Village Officer. 4. Issue.
✅ FAQ: 1. Q: Online? A: Yes. 2. Q: Documents? A: Death cert + ID. 3. Q: Fee? A: Standard. 4. Q: Validity? A: Continuous. 5. Q: Who verifies? A: VO. 6. Q: Is [Redacted] needed? A: Yes. 7. Q: Can I apply for pension? A: Yes, with this cert. 8. Q: If remarried? A: Not eligible. 9. Q: Track? A: Portal. 10. Q: Timeline? A: 1 week.
